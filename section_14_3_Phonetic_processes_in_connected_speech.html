3. Phonetic processes in connected speech

Hierarchical structure of syllables ( σ ). Key concepts:

- Onset (first consonant or consonant cluster)

- Nucleus (vowel)

- Coda (last consonant or consonant cluster)

Phone mic transcriptions, also known as broad transcriptions and written between slashes //, are used to represent words using phonemes, the minimal speech units that convey sense and meaning in a language.
